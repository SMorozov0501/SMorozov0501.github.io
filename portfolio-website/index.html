Project description

Tetris is a classic and iconic puzzle video game that challenges players to arrange falling blocks or "tetrominoes" into complete lines at the bottom of the screen. The objective is to clear as many lines as possible by strategically rotating and placing the descending shapes to create solid horizontal lines. When a line is successfully completed, it disappears, and the player earns points. As the game progresses, the blocks fall faster, increasing the challenge. The goal is to survive as long as possible and achieve the highest score. Tetris is known for its addictive and timeless gameplay, making it a beloved and enduring title in the world of video gaming.
